---
title: Link Instagram
description: "Link your Instagram account through Facebook to unlock advanced analytics in Flaire"
---

Connecting Instagram to Flaire requires a few one-time setup steps across Instagram, Facebook, and Flaire. Follow each step below in order — skip any you have already completed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
  ### Log in to Facebook & Instagram
    Make sure you can sign in to both platforms before continuing.

    1. Open [facebook.com](https://www.facebook.com) and log in to your Facebook account.
    ![Facebook login screen](/images/creator-insta/18.png)

    2. Open [instagram.com](https://www.instagram.com) and log in to your Instagram account.
    ![Instagram login screen](/images/creator-insta/19.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="">
  ### Switch to an Instagram Professional Account
    <Tip>Already have a **Creator** or **Business** account on Instagram? Skip ahead to Step 3.</Tip>

    1. On Instagram, click **More** (☰) in the bottom-left corner and open **Settings**.
    ![Instagram settings menu](/images/creator-insta/1.png)

    2. Navigate to **Account type and tools** and click **Switch to a professional account**.
    ![Switch to professional account option](/images/creator-insta/2.png)

    3. Select **Creator** as your account type.
    ![Selecting Creator account type](/images/creator-insta/4.png)

    4. Choose the category that best describes your content.
    ![Category selection screen](/images/creator-insta/6.png)

    5. Follow the remaining prompts to complete the switch.
    ![Professional account setup prompts](/images/creator-insta/7.png)

    <Note>Need more details? See Instagram's official guide: [Set up a professional account](https://help.instagram.com/502981923235522).</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="">
  ### Link Accounts in Meta Accounts Center
    Connect your Instagram and Facebook accounts so they can share data.

    1. Open the [Meta Accounts Center](https://accountscenter.facebook.com/).
    2. Go to the **Profiles** section and click **Add accounts**.
    ![Meta Accounts Center overview](/images/creator-insta/14.png)

    3. Click **Add Instagram account**.
    ![Add accounts in Accounts Center](/images/creator-insta/15.png)

    4. Follow the prompts to link your Instagram account to your Facebook account.

    <Note>Need more details? See Facebook's official guide: [Link Instagram and Facebook accounts](https://www.facebook.com/help/1148909221857370).</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="">
  ### Create a Facebook Page
    <Tip>Already have a Facebook Page? Skip ahead to Step 5.</Tip>

    1. On Facebook, click the **grid icon** (⊞) in the top bar and select the **Pages** tab.
    ![Facebook Pages tab in the grid menu](/images/creator-insta/9.png)

    2. Click **Create new Page** and fill in your page name, category, and other relevant details.
    ![Facebook Page creation form](/images/creator-insta/10.png)

    <Note>Need more details? See Facebook's official guide: [Create a Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/help/104002523024878).</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="">
  ### Link Instagram to Your Facebook Page
    1. Switch to your Facebook Page by clicking your profile icon and selecting your page in the dropdown.
    
    2. Then click the profile icon and select go to **Settings and privacy → Settings**.

    3. Navigate to **Linked accounts** in settings.
    ![Linked profiles setting on your Facebook Page](/images/creator-insta/20.png)

    4. Click **Connect account** under Instagram and follow the prompts to link your Instagram account.
    ![Connect Instagram account to Facebook Page](/images/creator-insta/21.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="">
  ### Connect Instagram in Flaire
    1. Log in to [Flaire](https://web.flaireapp.co).

    2. Open **Settings** from the sidebar.
    ![Flaire settings page](/images/creator-insta/5.png)

    3. Go to **Accounts and Rates** and click **Continue with Facebook** in the *Manage Instagram* section.
    ![Continue with Facebook button in Flaire](/images/creator-insta/8.png)

    4. Grant Flaire access to the relevant Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts so analytics can be pulled automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
