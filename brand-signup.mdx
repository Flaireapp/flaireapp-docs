## What you'll need
- A work email that matches your brand domain
- A PDF or image that proves you own or represent the brand (business registration, utility bill, or trademark certificate)
- Login credentials for at least one official brand social account (Instagram + Facebook, TikTok, or YouTube) so Flaire can verify ownership and pull analytics later
- Card or bank details to pay creators once you accept an agreement

## Create your profile
1. Visit [web.flaireapp.co](https://web.flaireapp.co) and select *Sign Up*.
2. Enter your email and a password.
3. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
4. Log back into Flaire and select **Brand** as your account type.
5. Complete your collaboration profile and add any mandatory disclosures you expect creators to follow.
6. Connect at least one official social media handle so Flaire can confirm the brand identity and unlock analytics for future posts.
