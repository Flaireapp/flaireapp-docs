# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Flaire ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website/platform. It describes what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

## What are cookies?
Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you visit websites. They are widely used to remember you and your preferences, either for a single visit or repeat visits.

## Why does Flaire use cookies?
We use cookies to:
- Ensure the platform functions smoothly and securely
- Recognize you when you log in
- Remember your preferences (like language or region)
- Analyze site usage to improve user experience
- Support marketing and communications efforts

## Types of cookies we use
- Essential cookies: Required for basic platform operations, such as logging in and managing sessions.
- Preference cookies: Store your preferences (language, theme).
- Analytics cookies: Collect information on how you use Flaire so we can enhance our service.
- Marketing cookies: Help us show relevant content and track the effectiveness of campaigns.

## Third-party cookies
Some cookies may be set by third-party services that appear on our pages (e.g., analytics companies, social networks). These third parties have their own privacy policies.

## Your choices
You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do so, however, you may need to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit the site and some services and functionalities may not work.
