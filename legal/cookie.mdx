This Cookie Policy explains how Flaire ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website/platform. It describes what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

## What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you visit websites. They are widely used to remember you and your preferences, either for a single visit or for repeat visits.

## Why Does Flaire Use Cookies?

We use cookies to:

- Ensure the platform functions smoothly and securely
- Recognize you when you log in
- Remember your preferences (like language or region)
- Analyze site usage to improve user experience
- Support marketing and communications efforts

## Types of Cookies We May Use

### Essential Cookies

Required for basic platform operations, such as logging in and managing sessions.

### Preference Cookies

Store your preferences (language, theme).

### Analytics Cookies

Collect information on how you use Flaire so we can enhance our service.

### Marketing Cookies

Help us show relevant content and track the effectiveness of campaigns.

### Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services that appear on our pages (e.g., analytics companies, social networks). These third parties have their own privacy policies.

## Your Choices

- You can delete all cookies that are already on your device
- You can set your browser to prevent cookies from being set. However, some platform features may not function as intended.

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or law. The updated policy will be posted on the Platform website with an effective date.
