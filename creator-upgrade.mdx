## Plan overview
| Plan | Campaigns you can apply to / month | Direct invitations / month | Included features |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | 1 | 1 | Core messaging, no platform/campaign fees |
| **Spark** | 3 | 5 | Campaign search, no platform/campaign fees |
| **Blaze** | 6 | 8 | Campaign search, Individual Analytics, no platform/campaign fees |
| **Supernova** | Unlimited | Unlimited | Campaign search, Individual Analytics, no platform/campaign fees |

- *Campaigns you can apply to* counts applications to public briefs and invited campaigns.
- *Direct invitations* are the private collaborations you can accept per month (brands can still message you, but you must free up an invite slot to accept).

## Keep your documents current
- Re-upload your ID and business info whenever something changes so payouts stay compliant.
- Update payout methods before you request advances to avoid holds.
- Maintain at least one connected social profile; analytics for invites/campaigns only appear when both sides keep their connections active.

## Working with advances
- The advance amount you request is paid right after a brand approves. Only ask for what you need to start production.
- Flaire releases the remaining balance when the brand approves or automatically seven days after you paste the live link (if the brand is silent).

## Taking a break
Toggle **Snooze applications** to stop receiving invitations temporarily. Existing campaigns stay active, and you can unsnooze anytime without losing your membership tier.
