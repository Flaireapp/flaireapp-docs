## What you'll need
- A valid email and mobile number for verification
- Login access to at least one social account you plan to showcase (Instagram + Facebook, TikTok, or YouTube) â€” connecting a social is required to finish signup
- Bank or payout details so you can receive advances and final payments

## Create your profile
1. Visit [web.flaireapp.co](https://web.flaireapp.co) and select *Sign Up*.
2. Enter your email and a password.
3. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
4. Log back into Flaire and select **Creator** as your account type.
5. Complete your profile with additional details.
6. Link at least one social account (more is better) so Flaire can verify your audience and unlock analytics once you start posting.
